## Research Experience

**Undergraduate Thesis — LesionNet** *(2025)*
Proposed a lightweight CNN for 7-class dermoscopic skin lesion classification on HAM10000 (10,015 images), avoiding heavy pretrained models. Handled class imbalance via augmentation and oversampling. Supervised by Asst. Prof. Salman Fazle Rabby, Sylhet Engineering College.

**Research Assistant — Dept. of EEE, Sylhet Engineering College** *(Dec 2024 – Jul 2025)*
ML-based wind turbine power forecasting and explainable PQD classification using SCADA data, classical ML (SVM, RF, XGBoost), and deep learning (MLP, 1D-CNN). Results published at IEEE ICCIT 2025.

**Research Trainee — Mahdy Research Academy** *(Jan – Dec 2024)*
Supervised by Dr. Mahdy Rahman Chowdhury, North South University. Advanced ML/DL foundations through Transformer architectures and XAI; produced a publication-ready manuscript and technical poster.
